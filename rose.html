Rose
Rose have a beautiful flower with petals and it symbolizes love. It is very common in valentine's, anniversary, and birthday that the man can give it to their lover. It has different colors like red, pink, blue, yellow, black, and many more. Every color have a different meaning, like the color red it means expression of love, feeling, and emotion. The rose has thorny branches so be careful if want to touch one of it.

How to plant?

Cut some stem on the rose and fill the small pot with soil. Then dig a hole for atleast 6 inches to plant the stem. Remove the leaves of the stem before you plant it, because it helps the rose to grow quickly. Rose is very sensitive so be careful after you plant it. After 4-10 days you will see some leaves, and when it grows, plant it in a big pot or in he ground that have direct sunlight at least 6 hours a day.
